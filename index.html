Ripple — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Ripple ("the app") is a daily synonym word game. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

Ripple does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All game data — including your scores, statistics, and game history — is stored locally on your device.

Game Center

Ripple supports Apple Game Center for leaderboards. If you choose to sign in to Game Center, your scores are submitted to Apple's Game Center service. This is managed entirely by Apple and subject to Apple's privacy policy. Game Center sign-in is optional and the app functions fully without it.

Notifications

Ripple may request permission to send you local push notifications to remind you to play the daily word. These notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device — no data is sent to any server. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

No Account Required

Ripple does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use the app. Game Center sign-in is optional.

No Analytics or Tracking

Ripple does not use any analytics services, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies. We do not track your usage, location, or behaviour.

No Third-Party Data Sharing

Ripple does not share any data with third parties. Game Center data is handled by Apple under their own privacy policy.

Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device using AsyncStorage:

— Daily game results (word, score, words found)
— Game statistics (games played, streaks, best score)
— This data never leaves your device and is deleted if you uninstall the app.

Children's Privacy

Ripple does not knowingly collect any data from children. The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at ripple.synonymgame@gmail.com
